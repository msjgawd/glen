Welcome to the Glen

A beautiful recreational property located in Maple Falls, Washington. The Glen is close to Mount Baker, and also a short drive to Bellingham. The Canadian border is less than half an hour away.

Enjoy your own retreat and property here at the Glen. A very desirable getaway destination, yet so close to home.
